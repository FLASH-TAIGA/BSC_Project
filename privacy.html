1. Who We Are

Flash Learning is a peer-to-peer academic tutoring platform based in Cameroon. We connect students with qualified tutors for online and onsite learning sessions. When you use our platform, you trust us with your information. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage it.

Contact: support@flashlearn.edu | +237-689 476 283

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: Full name, email address, password (stored as a secure hash), and role (student or tutor).
  • Profile data: Date of birth, profile photo, subjects of interest or expertise, qualifications, and bio.
  • Session data: Booked sessions including subject, date, time, mode (online or onsite), platform used, and amount paid.
  • Payment data: Mobile Money payment method (MTN MoMo or Orange Money) and phone number used for payment. We do not store full payment credentials.
  • Message data: In-platform messages exchanged between students and tutors.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, actions taken, and time spent on the platform (for improving the service).
3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on the platform.
  • To match students with suitable tutors based on subjects and preferences.
  • To process session bookings and payments.
  • To calculate and display earnings for tutors (85% of session fee) and platform revenue (15% service fee).
  • To enable in-platform messaging between students and tutors.
  • To send important notifications about your account or sessions.
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns and feedback.
  • To enforce our Terms of Use and prevent misuse.
4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on a secure MySQL database hosted on our server. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.
  • All API endpoints use prepared statements to prevent SQL injection.
  • Session authentication is managed via secure browser session storage.
  • Profile photos and uploaded materials are stored in a protected server directory.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out after each session.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Your data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Between users: Your name and profile information are visible to other users on the platform (e.g. students can see tutor profiles).
  • With administrators: Platform administrators can view user accounts, sessions, and messages for moderation and support purposes.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or in response to a valid legal request from authorities in Cameroon.
6. Cookies

Flash Learning uses browser session storage (not traditional cookies) to maintain your login session. This data is cleared automatically when you close your browser or log out. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. The platform may use Bootstrap and FontAwesome libraries loaded from local files, which do not set any cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view your profile and session history at any time from your dashboard.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information at any time from the My Profile section.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Note that session records may be retained for financial reporting purposes.
  • Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data in a readable format by contacting support.
  • Objection: You may object to certain uses of your data by contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@flashlearn.edu.

8. Children's Privacy

Flash Learning is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe a child under 16 has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately and we will remove the account and associated data promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:

  • Email: support@flashlearn.edu
  • Phone: +237-689 476 283
  • Online: Contact page

We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 2 business days.
